Okonomiyaki

Description

Savory japanese pancakes. Okonomi translates to "What you like" and yaki to "cook or grill".

Ingredients

  • cooked and choped chicken
  • thinly sliced cabbage
  • shredded carrots
  • green onions
  • eggs
  • all-purpose flour
  • soy sauce
  • chicken stock
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl beat eggs, flour, chicken stock and soy sauce together.
  2. In another bowl mix chicken, cabbage, carrots and green onions.
  3. Pour batter over the chicken mixture
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat
  5. put the batter in to the skillet and cover it
  6. cook for 4 minutes or until golden brown then flip it and cook for another 4 minutes or until cooked through
