Okonomiyaki

Description

Savory japanese pancakes. Okonomi translates to "What you like" and yaki to "cook or grill".

Ingredients

  • cooked and choped chicken
  • thinly sliced cabbage
  • shredded carrots
  • green onions
  • eggs
  • all-purpose flour
  • soy sauce
  • chicken stock
  • vegetable oil
