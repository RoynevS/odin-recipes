Chinese Buffet Green Beans

Description

Side dish that goes well with all Asian food. Simple to make but rich in flavor.

Ingredients

  • oil
  • garlic
  • fresh green beans
  • oyster sauce
  • sugar
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and stir until edges begin to brown.
  3. Add the green beans and stir until the are beginning to soften
  4. Add oyster sauce, soy sauce and sugar
  5. Stir until desired tenderness of the beans is reached.
